What is Artificial Intelligence?

To put it in simple terms, AI is a branch of computer science that is dedicated to creating intelligent machines that work and react like humans.



How Does AI Work?

AI's work through 'Machine Learning' which is essentially how computers recognize patterns and make decisions without being explicitly programmed. Through trail and error, as well as practice, a machine is able to take in data of all kinds and create predictions for noticing differences between, lets say a car and a bike. Deep learning is another method AI's use to develop their intelligence. This method is Machine Learning, just with a considerable boost in processing power and speed, giving the output from an AI to have minimal error.



Types of AI:

  1. Reactive Machines: Purely reactive and do not hold the ability to form memories or use past experiences to make decisions.
  2. Limited Memory: Uses past experiences and present data to make a decision. Self driving cars are an example of this AI.
  3. Theory of Mind: Can socialize and understand human emotions

Example of how AI is used in Business:

www.personal.ai

Personal AI uses documents and information to create an AI Powered Assistant, to assist the user in their business. The stand out feature they have is their platform allows for the answers the AI gives to be based on past information taken from the user. This creates a more personal user experience for the person using the AI compared to someone who would use an alternative chat bot.
